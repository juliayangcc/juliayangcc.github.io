Editorial Artwork

These are pieces I have made for Boston College's Gavel Newspaper. I learned how to create impactful and visually engaging illustrations from just a brief summary of an article's subject matter and within tight deadlines. Softwares that I used include: Procreate, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, and more. Some illustrations are interactive and link to the corresponding published article! An "unclickable" illustration means the article was scrapped and never made it to publication.
